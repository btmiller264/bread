The Art of Breadmaking

My name is Braden Miller, and one of the things I have always wanted is to be good at making bread (also the time to make bread!). I think good bread can make or break a meal - some of the best meals I have had have been because the bread is absolutley stellar. So, in preperation for the time when I have the time to learn, I made this little website. I compiled some tips on first time breadmaking that I looked up, as well as putting down some basic and easy recipes that I have. Feel free to take a look - I will add more recipes soon!
