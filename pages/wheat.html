Honey Wheat Bread Recipe

This one is from my aunt who lives in Arkansas!

Ingredients

  • 6 cups warm water
  • 2/3 cup oil
  • 2/3 cup honey
  • 2 TB Salt
  • 6-8 cups Wheat Flour
  • 3 TB Yeast
  • Bread Flour

Directions

  1. Mix together all ingredients except for the yeast and flour in a bowl. Add the wheat flour and mix until the dough is about the consistency of cake batter.
  2. Add the 3 TB of yeast and then enough bread flour til the dough starts to stick together and clean the sides of the mixing bowl.
  3. Take out the dough and knead it for about 6 minutes.
  4. Seperate into 5 sepereate loaves and place in greased bread pans.
  5. Put the dough in a warm place to rise for about 30 to 60 minutes. It will about double in size. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  6. After the dough is done rising, put the bread into the oven, but drop the temperature down to 325 degrees F. Bake for about 25-30 minutes, or until the internal temperature of the bread is 180 degrees.
  7. Remove from pans and let cool before slicing and serving!
  8. Enjoy - I used to love this with butter and honey in the morning!
